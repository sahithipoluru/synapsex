FAQ

What is SynapseX?

An autonomous, self-learning AI platform for end-to-end market analysis, signal generation, and execution optimization.

Does it self-learn?

Yes. Post-trade attribution and performance drift detection feed continuous model & feature weight refinement.

Is it fully autonomous?

Execution & signal generation can be automated. Human oversight sets guardrails, objectives, and risk bounds.

Which markets are supported?

Designed to adapt across equities, crypto, and FX; initial public release will prioritize liquid digital assets and major FX pairs.

How is my data handled?

All processing is designed to be pipeline-isolated. No external sharing or monetization of proprietary data.
